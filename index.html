Victor Aduragbemi Dada

Victor Dada is a Nigerian-born music and computing student. He completed his primary education at Calvary International School before attending Preston International School for secondary education, where he earned a preliminary grade from Muson, Nigeria’s music board. While at Preston, Victor was an active member of the school choir and brass band, which performed “Fairy Odd Parent (Timmy Turner)” as the final presentation for the graduating class of 2020/21. After secondary school, he enrolled in a graphic design course, gaining skills in Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and CorelDRAW. Victor later moved to the UK with his family to pursue computer science studies, completing a BTEC Level 3 Extended Certificate and a National Diploma in Computing. He has recently gained admission to study computer science at the University of Derby. Victor began playing piano at age seven and discovered his passion for the trumpet during his time at Preston, practicing diligently ever since. In December 2024, he performed “O Thou That Tellest” as an accompanist, and on August 17, 2025, he played first trumpet in the brass presentation titled “America Patrol.” You can view some of his pictures in the Pictures tab and watch videos in the Media tab.
